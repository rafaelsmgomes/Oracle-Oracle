Intro

Some years ago, we recognized that we had to evolve from a product orientated company with exceptional products in to a provider of exceptional services. One that continually helps our customers achieve their goals through today’s and tomorrow’s innovations.

To make this transformation a reality, we had to move our entire business to the cloud.

Our vision was to build the first cloud enterprise that would connect enterprise lines of business into a single cloud entity. The second generation of Oracle Cloud delivers applications, platforms, and infrastructure, all as a service and made this vision possible. By implementing Oracle Cloud across Oracle, we’d be an end-to-end cloud provider, and an end-to-end cloud enterprise.

If you’d like to come along, we’d like to share our experience with you, so that you can benefit the same way we did: cost savings, accelerated growth, and increased customer and employee satisfaction rates.

We’ll share our results and lessons learned—and show you our proven roadmap. We’ve been there and done that.

Here’s what you can expect:

  • Maximize efficiency and effectiveness. Free up resources by adding integrated automation across your entire business, so you can shift your focus to high-value activities that drive success, increase cross-functional collaboration, and save money.
  • Make the smartest decisions. Gain end-to-end insights into your business and enhance your decision-making with smart data, AI, and machine learning.
  • Outpace change. Stay ahead of ever-changing trends with quarterly innovation provided to you for your entire business.
  • Delight your customers and employees. Realize increased satisfaction, engagement, and loyalty from those you serve with consumer-centered experiences from end to end.

Approach

1 Laying the Groundwork for the End-to-End Cloud Journey

Strategy was key. We began by listening to our customers and employees—and internalizing their needs. Customers and employees both wanted a strategic partner who could help them achieve their goals. Once we knew the end game, we developed a four-step process.

  • Define To determine how we would achieve our goals, we needed to reimagine the end-to-end customer experience as well as the employee experience. Our employees are as important to us as our customers—we depend on them to help us deliver an exceptional customer experience.
  • Design To focus on what matters most to our customers and employees, we designed our process to seamlessly flow across our four key lines of business—finance, human resources, supply chain, and customer experience.
  • Engage We wanted our employees to possess a customer-first mindset, to translate their insights into business guidance, and to communicate with and influence our leadership team. To do this, they needed deep business acumen and the kinds of interpersonal skills required to impact decision-making at the highest levels of the business. This was key to our engagement process.
  • Execute To become the strategic partner our customers and employees needed, we streamlined our processes and focused on the core business areas that would give us the most traction, quickly—using Oracle Cloud to power our transformation.

Transforming the Finance Experience

Our finance organization provides valuable data, manages risk, and helps ensure that the entire business operates efficiently. To enable our end-to-end cloud journey, we had to empower our leadership with real-time insight—and translate that insight into business guidance to help them make the right decisions quickly.

For the purposes of speed and efficiency, we focused on automating three key areas of finance that would have the most impact right out of the gate: reporting, planning, and expense submission and approval. For reporting activities (such as accounting close and management reporting) and planning activities (such as modeling and forecasting), we achieved fully automated processes that enable real-time reporting and data availability. Similarly, for procurement and expense reporting, we automated the experience from end to end—from point of transaction through expense submission, review, and approval.

4 Automate

Automated approximately 20,000 monthly balance sheet reconciliations using Oracle Account Reconciliation Cloud Service. This gives the team global, real-time dashboard reporting visibility throughout the close period.
